Services

Tailored offensive security solutions designed to test systems, uncover vulnerabilities, and prepare organizations against sophisticated threats.

Penetration Testing

Penetration testing is a proactive service where we simulate real-world cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in your infrastructure, systems, and applications. We assess the security of your systems before attackers can exploit them..

Malware Development

The malware development service tests the effectiveness of your security systems by creating custom malware designed to evade detection. This helps strengthen your defenses by identifying gaps in your detection and mitigation capabilities.

Red Team Simulation

Red teaming simulates advanced, coordinated cyberattacks to assess your organisation's defense capabilities in high-stress, real-world scenarios. I replicate the techniques, tactics and procedures used by sophisticated cybercriminals and state-sponsored hackers.

Offensive Phishing Operations

The offensive phishing operations service evaluates your organisation’s resilience against targeted social engineering attacks by crafting and deploying sophisticated phishing campaigns. These custom-built operations mimic real-world tactics used by attackers to trick employees, bypass defenses, and infiltrate systems, helping you identify vulnerabilities in both technology and human awareness.

WiFi Exploitation

The WiFi exploitation service assesses your organisation’s wireless network security by simulating advanced attacker techniques. Using cutting-edge tools and methodologies, I conduct targeted attacks—such as rogue access point deployment, traffic interception, and credential harvesting—to expose vulnerabilities in your WiFi infrastructure, encryption, and user behavior. This service helps you strengthen defenses against real-world wireless threats.
