Services

I provide a wide range of services to enhance your organisation’s security posture and tackle cybersecurity challenges head-on. These services include:

1. Penetration Testing: Simulating Real-World Cyberattacks

Penetration testing is a proactive service where we simulate real-world cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in your infrastructure, systems, and applications. I will assess the security of your systems before attackers can exploit them.

How it works:

  • Reconnaissance and Footprinting: I will gather information about your organization’s network and systems to find potential entry points.
  • Vulnerability Scanning: I will identify known weaknesses with both automated tools and manual techniques.
  • Exploitation: I will attempt to exploit discovered vulnerabilities to assess the severity of the threat.
  • Post-Exploitation: I will analyse what further damage could occur if an attacker gained access.
  • Reporting and Mitigation Recommendations: As part of a package, I will provide a detailed report with actionable recommendations for improving your security posture.

Why it matters:

Penetration testing allows your organisation to identify and address vulnerabilities before they are exploited, ensuring that your systems are secure and resilient to real-world attacks.

2. Red Teaming Simulation: Advanced Adversarial Tactics

Red teaming simulates advanced, coordinated cyberattacks to assess your organization's defense capabilities in high-stress, real-world scenarios. I emulate the techniques, tactics and procedures used by sophisticated cybercriminals and state-sponsored hackers.

How it works:

  • Threat Simulation: I will replicate the Techniques, Tactics, and Procedures (TTPs) of advanced adversaries.
  • Scenario Planning: I will customise attacks based on different types of threat actors, from cybercriminals to nation-state actors.
  • Blended Attack Methodology: I use various attack vectors like social engineering, physical penetration, and network exploitation.
  • Response Evaluation: This will stress-test your organisation's detection and response processes during an active, real-time attack.
  • Reporting and Post-Engagement Debrief: As part of a package, I will provide a comprehensive report on the tactics used, defensive gaps, and how to improve security resilience.

Why it matters:

Red team exercises assess how your people, processes, and technology perform under pressure and provide key insights to strengthen your overall security posture.

3. Malware Development: Enhancing Detection and Mitigation Capabilities

The malware development service tests the effectiveness of your security systems by creating custom malware designed to evade detection. This helps strengthen your organisations defenses by identifying gaps in your detection capabilities.

How it works:

  • Tailored Malware Creation: I will develop malware specifically designed for your network environment.
  • Evasion Techniques: I will implement techniques such as obfuscation and encryption to bypass traditional detection systems.
  • Bypassing Security Systems: I will test how easily the malware can bypass firewalls, antivirus, and intrusion prevention systems.
  • Simulated Attacks: I will deploy the malware in a controlled environment to observe how it spreads and identify weaknesses.
  • Recommendations and Improvements: I will provide actionable insights to improve detection and response capabilities based on the findings.

Why it matters:

Testing how your organisation's security systems react to custom malware allows you to fortify your defenses and ensure that your organisation can effectively detect and respond to real-world threats.
